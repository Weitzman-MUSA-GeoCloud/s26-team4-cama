How Assessments Work

The OPA determines the market value of every property in Philadelphia annually. Values are based on comparable sales, property characteristics, and local market conditions.

Appeal Your Assessment

If you believe your assessment is inaccurate, file a First Level Review (FLR) with the OPA or appeal to the Board of Revision of Taxes (BRT). Deadlines apply.

Tax Relief Programs

You may qualify for the Homestead Exemption, Senior Citizen Tax Freeze, or the Low-income Senior Citizen Real Estate Tax Freeze — reducing your annual tax burden.
